# Product-Pricing-Profitability-Dashboard
An interactive dashboard that analyzes product-level pricing, revenue, cost, and profit performance. It highlights the impact of discounts on profit margins and identifies top and low-performing products. Designed to support data-driven pricing decisions and improve overall profitability.

#Businesses often struggle to understand how pricing and discount strategies impact profitability. This dashboard analyzes product-level pricing performance to identify profit drivers and loss areas.

#Key KPIS
.Total Revenue
.Total Cost
.Total Profit
.Profit Margin %
.Discount Impact
.Contribution to Total Profit
.Average Order Value (AOV)

# Business Questions / Challenges
Q-1).Which products and categories generate the highest revenue and profit?
By Using Visual Charts we can understand that Meals Categories and Pizzas have collected most Revenue and Profit

Q-2).How do discounts affect profitability at the product and category level?
Discounts have a significant impact on profitability, as they directly reduce revenue while costs remain largely unchanged. From the analysis, products and categories sold without discounts consistently generate higher profit margins, whereas heavily discounted products show reduced or even negative profitability. This indicates that while discounts may boost sales volume, excessive discounting can erode overall profit, especially at the category level.
